I am a postdoctoral fellow at Harvard University, working with Minlan Yu and Michael Mitzenmacher. Before coming to Harvard, I was a Ph.D. candidate at the CS department of Technion under the supervision of Prof. Roy Friedman. I got my B.Sc and M.Sc from the same department. I am primarily interested in algorithms for networking systems and measurement.
